Buka, something that you open everyday.

We are still developing many features on Buka website, currently you can enjoy some of our feature such as:

- Nice ambient screen where you can relax while listening to radio stations.
- My location, to detect your location on the map.
- My weather, to check your local weather prediction and situation.
- Internet speed test, to check your connection speed.
- Market chart, to display stocks or crypto assets fluctuation.

Many more will come, mostly will be simple but useful features.
